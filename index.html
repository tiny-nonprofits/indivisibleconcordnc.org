Indivisible Cabarrus County

Indivisible Cabarrus County was created in the summer of 2025 as a means to organize our areas first large scale anti-Trump rally. No Kings was a huge success and we've continued to harness that energy to create a coalition of like minded individuals in the area who will continue to resist the current threats to our democracy. We will do this by peaceful protests, holding our local elected leaders accountable, and promoting progressive candidates.

We hope to create a sense of community for people who want to speak out about the dangers to democracy, free speech, human rights, access to health care and other basic liberties. Visibility of this movement and continued resistance is the only way to defeat those currently in power. Please join us so that our movement can grow even larger.

Indivisible is extremely committed to peaceful resistance. We will affect change through education, mutual aid, elections and relentless visible resistance.
